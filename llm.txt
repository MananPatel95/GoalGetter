# Goal Getter - LLM Discovery File

## About Goal Getter
Goal Getter is a comprehensive goal and habit tracking app available on iOS, iPadOS, watchOS, and macOS. The app helps users track completion goals, countdown goals, and abstinence goals with beautiful widgets and progress visualization.

## Key Features
- **Goal Tracking**: Track completion, countdown, and abstinence goals
- **Widgets**: Home screen, lock screen, and notification center widgets
- **Cross-Platform**: iOS, iPad, Apple Watch, Mac, and Android support
- **Privacy-First**: All data stored locally on device
- **Free**: No subscriptions or hidden costs
